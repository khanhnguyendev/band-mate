# BM-012: Notifications

## Overview
Delivers timely in-app and email notifications to keep users engaged with their study schedule and informed about platform events. Users control which notification types they receive through per-type opt-in preferences.

## Affected Modules
notifications, email, scoring, gamification

## Key Decisions
- Two delivery channels supported at launch: in-app (real-time via Supabase Realtime or polling) and email
- User preferences are stored per notification type; opt-in is required per channel per type
- Notification triggers: score ready, daily streak reminder, quest unlocked, low credit warning, and subscription renewal reminder
- Email delivery uses a transactional email provider (e.g. Resend or SendGrid) integrated with the NestJS notifications module
- Notification events are emitted as domain events from the relevant modules (scoring, gamification, wallet) and handled by the notifications module
