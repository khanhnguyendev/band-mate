# BM-011: Plans, Pricing and Wallet

## Overview
Defines the subscription tier model, Stripe-based checkout flow, and the credit wallet system that gates AI-scored practice. A double-entry ledger with idempotency guarantees ensures reliable credit accounting across reserve, consume, and refund operations.

## Key Decisions
- Three subscription tiers: Free (limited credits), Starter, and Pro
- Payments are handled through Stripe Checkout; webhook events drive subscription state transitions
- Credit wallet supports three operations: reserve (hold on submit), consume (finalise on score delivery), and refund (release if scoring fails)
- All ledger writes require an idempotency key to prevent double-crediting or double-debiting on retries
- Bonus credit campaigns can be issued server-side and are recorded as distinct ledger entry types
